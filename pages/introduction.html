Quadcopter

The term quadcopter or quadrotor comes from the four narrow chord propellers used by the air vehicle in order to maneuver in air. The quadcopter uses these four propellers to push the air downwards in order to generate lift force. It maneuvers by varying the speeds of these four motors.
